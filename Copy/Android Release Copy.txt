Title
Sappy Secrets

Short description
Solve neighborhood mysteries in this real-world romp through a forest of lies.

Full description
As Agent Almond of the Oaken Empire, dive crown-first into a twisted thicket of espionage as you save your nation from a mysterious treacherous tree.

Sappy Secrets is an augmented reality spy adventure game brought to you by jamesw6811.

It is played using your cell phone's GPS and earbuds. It can be played walking or running.

NOTE: Requires wired or wireless earbuds/earphones with a dedicated play/pause button.

Features
- Run or walk around your real-world neighborhood discovering and solving mysteries.
- Build your arborous spy network by interacting with many types of trees.
- Strategize your next steps for saving the Oaken Empire and collecting sweet rewards.
